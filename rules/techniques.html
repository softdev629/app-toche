SPOON

Here is where you start. Simple and consistent, but far from spectacular.

BACKSPIN

The arm moves the same way as in the spoon, but your hand is facing down. The wrist moves upward at the last moment to give it that killer spin.

  • TOP

BASKETBALL

Same motion as in basketball, but keep your feet on the platform when you throw. No jumping allowed.


  • TOP

SLAM

It’s like throwing a stone. The speed and sound when it hits the target have a subconscious effect of intimidating your opponent. It’s also a lovely technique to burn your friends.


  • TOP

EAGLE

This is the arty form of Toche. It’s elegant and swift, but hard to master. The arms swings backwards and covers a 180 degree trajectory.
