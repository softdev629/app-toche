Penalties

Penalties are four extra throws granted to a player because the opponent broke a rule. When you get penalised, let your opponent throw the 4 balls of his/her round. Then, QUICKLY pick up his/her balls and bring them to him/her. Your opponent will get to throw 4 more balls (the penalties) and then the game proceeds as usual.

STEPPING ON THE LINE

Players must throw standing behind the platform line. Standing on the line is penalised—even if it’s just 1 cm. Clip your toenails if you must, but stay behind the line.

STEPPINGON THE LOOP

When you are running around the loop, be careful not to hit any of the cones or other type of barrier; or to step on the line. Don’t cut any corners. You will be penalised if you do so.

SWINGING

When throwing the balls, you can’t swing your arms to calibrate how you throw. You can only make one movement forward. You can start the motion by stretching your arm forward, then pull back and swing forward in one single motion.

THREE WITH ONE HAND

Throwing three balls with the right hand, instead of just two, is a penalty. Throwing only one with the right is also a penalty.

COUNTING ERROR

    You will be penalised if you:
  • Forget to count right after throwing the ball and seeing the result.
  • Make a mistake or pronounce the number wrong.
  • Don’t count loud enough.
  • Take too long in calculating (i.e. your brain must send the order to your mouth to utter the number right after the ball impacts the target or the floor. If the order gets lost in the way, too bad, it’s a penalty!)

If you arrive to the cheering area after your opponent has thrown the first ball with her left hand, you will be penalised. She will finish throwing the last ball with the left hand, then you will run to pick up the four balls and deliver them to her with a humble smile.

HOOLIGAN

You are allowed to cheer for yourself and annoy the hell out of your opponent. However, you will be penalised if you:


  • Use bad language to insult your opponent.
  • Falsely claim a rule violation.
  • Say any number (it confuses your opponent).
  • Stand out of the Cheering Area before your opponent throws the fourth ball.

If you incur in hooliganism three times in a match, you will be disqualified automatically and your opponent will win the game by a score of 25-0 songs (inputing a 10-0 score for each song).


Albeit not being gracious, farting is not considered hooligan behaviour, and so, it’s not penalised... It won’t make you many friends, but it can give you an edge—specially if you’re playing indoors.

TOO TIRED

If you are too tired to finish a song, you can give up, but you must remain making the loser sign on your forehead until the end of the song.


Your opponent will continue throwing, running, picking up, running and throwing and all the points will count. If your opponent stops running or makes a mistake in the count, the score will be frozen at that point. Both can rest until the next song begins.


If you stop doing the loser sign, your opponent can hit you with the balls and get 2 points for each impact.

TURTLE DELIVERY

Whenever you get penalised or get burned you have to pick up your opponents balls and deliver them quickly. If you take your time to pick them up or drag your feet to deliver them, it is another penalty.


Being penalised sucks, but incurring in a double penalty is the worst thing that can happen to you. Your opponent will get to throw 2 extra rounds, and catch her breath, while you will be running and delivering the balls like a fool.


So, whenever you get penalised, do yourself a favour and be quick about it.


After you pick up the balls for your opponent, you have to run and put them in her hands gently.

FORGET PENALTIES

If you get penalised and forget about it, say you run to the cheering area instead of waiting by the barrier, you will be penalised.


Once again, this is something worth slapping yourself about. It is practically giving away the song to your opponent.


So, whenever you get penalised, go and stand by the barrier. Since carrying 8 balls may be difficult, you can throw yours to the cheering area and get ready to pick your opponents balls quickly.
